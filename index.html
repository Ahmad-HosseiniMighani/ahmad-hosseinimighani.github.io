About Me

I am a developer who has taken the red pill and seen how deep the rabbit hole goes. The digital world is my playground, and code is my weapon of choice. My passion for technology began early; I started coding at 13, which gives me a deep, foundational understanding of software development.

I'm a Full-Stack / Front-End Engineer with 7+ years of professional experience building and leading high-traffic web and mobile projects. With years of experience in both front-end and back-end development, I create digital experiences that challenge reality and push boundaries.

I have a proven track record leading teams, including successfully managing the development of panhomestores.com with a team of 20+ engineers at Scandiweb, consistently delivering under heavy deadlines.

My entrepreneurial background includes co-founding fitmoteam and founding TeamSpeak32 at the age of 16. I also serve as a dedicated mentor of junior developers.

I possess a strong skill set in technologies including React.js, Next.js, Node.js, PHP, GraphQL, and cloud-based deployments.

With a strong mix of coding expertise, team leadership, and client communication, I am now focused on bringing my technical and leadership skills to build and scale innovative products. When I'm not coding, I'm exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or questioning the nature of my reality.
